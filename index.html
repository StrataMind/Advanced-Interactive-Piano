🎯 Basic Fundamentals

🎹 Piano Basics

Learn about piano keys, white and black keys, and basic hand positioning.

  • • 88 keys: 52 white + 36 black
  • • Pattern: 7 white keys per octave
  • • Hand position and posture

🎼 Musical Notes

Understand note names, octaves, and how they relate to piano keys.

  • • Note names: C D E F G A B
  • • Sharp (#) and flat (♭) notes
  • • Octave system (C0 to C8)

⏱️ Rhythm & Timing

Learn about beats, tempo, and basic rhythm patterns.

  • • BPM (Beats Per Minute)
  • • Quarter, half, whole notes
  • • Count: 1-2-3-4

🎵 Scales & Chords

🎼 Major Scale

The foundation of Western music. Pattern: W-W-H-W-W-W-H

  • • C Major: C D E F G A B
  • • No sharps or flats
  • • Whole (W) and Half (H) steps

🎹 Basic Chords

Learn to play your first chords: triads and basic progressions.

  • • C Major: C-E-G
  • • F Major: F-A-C
  • • G Major: G-B-D

🎵 Chord Progressions

Popular chord sequences used in thousands of songs.

  • • I-V-vi-IV (Pop progression)
  • • ii-V-I (Jazz standard)
  • • Practice with both hands

🎯 Practice Tips

🕐 Daily Practice

Build a consistent practice routine for steady improvement.

  • • Start with 15-30 minutes daily
  • • Warm up with scales
  • • Focus on one song at a time

🎯 Technique Tips

Proper technique prevents injury and improves sound quality.

  • • Curved fingers, relaxed wrists
  • • Practice slowly first
  • • Use metronome for timing

🎵 Song Learning

Step-by-step approach to learning new pieces.

  • • Learn hands separately first
  • • Practice difficult sections slowly
  • • Gradually increase tempo

📖 How to Use
🎹 Playing: Click keys, use keyboard shortcuts, or tab to navigate and press Enter/Space
⌨️ Shortcuts: 9 keys: A S D F G H J K L (white keys C D E F G A B C D)
🎛️ Controls: Change octaves with +/- buttons to access all notes. ADSR envelope, multiple instruments, reverb, delay, filter
🎵 Musical Features: Extended chords, scales, chord progressions, metronome
🎧 Recording: Record performances, save as WAV or MIDI files
📱 Accessibility: Full keyboard navigation, screen reader support, touch-friendly
